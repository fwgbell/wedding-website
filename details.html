Good to know

Dress code

Traditional Morning Dress is warmly encouraged for those who own it.

For guests without morning dress, formal wedding attire is requested (e.g. dark suits with ties for gentlemen, and elegant daywear or formal dresses for ladies).

Weather permitting, the drinks reception will take place on the lawn, so please consider your footwear accordingly.

Getting to the reception

The Grange is a short drive from the church, with ample parking available. For those who prefer, the route can also be walked in around 15 minutes along a track. Please bear in mind the track is rough and uneven, so carefully consider your footwear if you plan to walk.

Coach service

A coach service will be provided from Winchester to the ceremony, from the ceremony to the reception, and returning to Winchester at the end of the evening. Please indicate in your RSVP if you would like to make use of this.

Children

We kindly request that our celebration be adults only; the only children in attendance will be those in the wedding party.

Children are welcome to attend the garden party the following day.
