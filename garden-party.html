Happily Ever After Garden Party

Join us for a relaxed second-day celebration.

When

Sunday, 20 September 2026 · Midday

Where

Denbigh
Shepherds Lane, Compton
Winchester, SO21 2AD

The day after the wedding, we would love to welcome you to Flora's family home for a relaxed afternoon in the garden. Join us for chilled drinks, a delicious open-fire buffet lunch and plenty of time to unwind and enjoy the celebration together.

Schedule

  • 12:00 PM Arrival
  • 1:00 PM Lunch is served
  • 2:30 PM The cutting of our cake!
  • 4:30 PM Afternoon continues / goodbye hugs

What to expect

Food & drink

Beautifully prepared dishes served fresh from the flames, as well as all you can eat pancakes for dessert!! Wash it down with some refreshing drinks and enjoy the sunshine in the garden.

Dress code

Smart casual garden party attire. Please bring a layer if it's breezy, and note that the event will take place on grass, so appropriate footwear is recommended.

Getting there

See the map and directions below for how to find us and where to park.

Directions & Parking

Follow Shepherds Lane until you are almost at the very end. You will pass Flora’s family home on your left, marked in red on the map, please don’t park here. Just before the lane ends, turn left down a track, highlighted in blue. Keep an eye out for groomsmen who will be on hand to guide you in. Park in the field at the end of the track, also marked in blue.
